Frequently asked

How does the daily plan work?

Align builds you a fresh five-part plan every day covering your neck, shoulders, upper back, core, and hips. Complete it to keep your streak going and earn XP.

Do I need an account or email to use Align?

No. Align creates an anonymous account tied to a random identifier on your device. We never ask for your email, phone number, or real name.

What do I get with Pro?

Pro unlocks 20 guided programs, the ability to build your own custom programs from the full exercise library, and detailed progress reports and insights.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple App Store account. You can manage or cancel anytime in your device Settings under your Apple ID, or at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open Settings in the app and tap "Restore purchases." Your Pro access is tied to your Apple ID.

How do I delete my data?

Open Settings in the app and choose "Delete account & data." This permanently removes your data from your device and our cloud storage.

Will the exercises help a medical condition?

Align is for general wellness and is not medical advice. If you have pain or a medical condition, talk to a healthcare professional before starting, and stop if anything hurts.
